In 2018, under the leadership of Christian Saffon, Jonnathan Garzon, Marcela Gonzales, Jaime Duque and Jhoham Rojas, ADC was born as a company that provides services such as audits and inspections of aeronautical documentation for lessors around the world. In less than two years, the company grew considerably, increasing its portfolio with more robust services for review of unplanned and routine maintenance, scanning and indexing of technical documentation. Currently ADC has a team of highly qualified Aeronautical Engineers with the necessary experience to provide a high-quality product to the client.

Learn more

Aircraft records are a fundamental part of the asset, being essential to carry out traceability in the event of a lease transition or to validate the airworthiness of the aircraft. Successfully auditing and maintaining the technical records guarantees that the value of the asset is maintained by complying with the conditions of the contract, sale, or lease of both the aircraft and its components.

Learn more

We have a solid track record in auditing aircraft technical documentation, being our main priority to guarantee the quality of our services with total transparency to meet customer requirements.

Our work team can assist the user's needs while maintaining the operating value of their assets through:

  • Review of aeronautical technical records.
  • Audit and control of the information required in the technical documentation of the aircraft.
  • Classification/Categorization of digital technical records.
  • Organization and traceability of the technical records that allow the operational continuity of the aircraft.
  • Digitization of aeronautical technical records.
